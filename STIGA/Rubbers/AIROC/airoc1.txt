AIROC ASTRO M TABLE TENNIS RUBBER

In Airoc Astro we have multiplied the amount of microscopic air bubbles (Oxygen Capsule System) in the sponge creating more intense catapult effect and speed. The topsheet works as an energy transfer between the sponge and the ball which provides every stroke with precision and power. Airoc Astro is developed togehter with members of the Chinese National Team.

The Medium sponge is optimized to produce an elastic feel when striking the ball.
The OCS-Sponge provides the player with catapult effect.
Perfect choice for players who wants long trajectory shots.
Perfect rubber for offensive attackers who plays with spin, feeling and sound.
