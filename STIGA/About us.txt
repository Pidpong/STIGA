STIGA Sports AB, a global company with partners in over 100 countries. The classic Swedish table tennis company with both hobby and professional products. In 1944, STIGA started its production of table tennis products and is today established as one of the world's largest brands of table tennis. STIGA also offers a wide range of outdoor and indoor sports items with classics like SnowracerR and the Play-Off table hockey games. The head office is located in Eskilstuna.
